About Kovecreek Inc.

Kovecreek Inc. is a global apparel sourcing and import company built to serve modern fashion brands with precision, accountability, and scale.

Our Mission

To simplify global garment sourcing while maintaining the highest standards of quality, ethics, and transparency.

What We Do

We manage the full apparel production lifecycle — from factory selection and sampling to bulk manufacturing, quality control, and international logistics.

Where We Operate

Our sourcing network spans India, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and select regions across Asia, supported by on-ground teams.

Who We Work With

We partner with independent fashion labels, large retailers, private-label brands, and emerging designers worldwide.
